For your portfolio, an Open Banking Dashboard is the strongest project because it aligns directly with your fintech, microservices, JWT, RBAC, Open Banking, audit logging, and transaction-processing experience. If executed properly, it will look like something built by a Senior Frontend Engineer, Product Engineer, or Founding Engineer rather than a tutorial project.
The goal should not be "a banking app UI."
The goal should be:
"A modern Open Banking Operations Platform used by banks, fintechs, auditors, compliance officers, and support teams."

Product Requirements Document (PRD)
Product Name
FinFlow Open Banking Platform
Product Vision
A modern Open Banking platform that allows customers, bank administrators, compliance officers, and auditors to manage accounts, payments, transactions, API consents, and operational monitoring from a unified dashboard.
The platform demonstrates:
* Enterprise UX
* Data-heavy interfaces
* Fintech workflows
* Regulatory compliance
* Role-based experiences
* Real-time transaction monitoring
________________


Problem Statement
Traditional banking dashboards focus only on balances and transactions.
Open Banking introduces additional requirements:
* Customer consent management
* Third-party provider access
* API monitoring
* Fraud detection
* Compliance auditing
* Permission management
* Operational visibility
Financial institutions need a centralized dashboard for managing these activities.
